Eclipse Curry Bowl

A warm chickpea curry with coconut milk, tomato, and rice. It adds a deep, comforting flavor profile to the archive and fits the moody eclipse visual theme beautifully.

Time Module

35 min

Skill Level

Medium

Dish Signal

A bold comfort bowl with depth

This recipe strengthens the archive with another full meal option. Its warm color tones also help the home page card grid avoid feeling too cool or monochrome.

Curry Chickpea Comfort

Ingredient Vault

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 cup boiled chickpeas
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • Salt to taste

Cooking Sequence

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and cook the onion until soft and light golden.
  2. Add garlic and tomato, then cook until the tomato starts to break down.
  3. Mix in chickpeas, turmeric, curry powder, and salt.
  4. Pour in coconut milk and a small splash of water.
  5. Let the curry simmer until the sauce thickens slightly.
  6. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  7. Serve hot with cooked rice in a bowl.
