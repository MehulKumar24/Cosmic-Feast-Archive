Solar Spice Soup

A bright and warming soup made with tomato, carrot, lentils, and spices. It adds a bold color contrast to the site and gives variety among the recipe pages.

Time Module

40 min

Skill Level

Easy

Dish Signal

Warm, bright, and easy to make

This recipe is useful for your assignment because it has a simple method, a clean ingredient list, and a visually strong color palette that suits the sci-fi fantasy theme.

Ingredient Vault

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup red lentils
  • 3 cups vegetable stock
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Cooking Sequence

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a pot and cook the onion until soft.
  2. Add carrots and tomatoes, then stir for a few minutes.
  3. Mix in the lentils, turmeric, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour in the vegetable stock and bring the soup to a boil.
  5. Lower the heat and cook until the carrots and lentils are fully tender.
  6. Blend the soup carefully until smooth or leave it slightly textured.
  7. Serve hot with bread or a light garnish on top.
