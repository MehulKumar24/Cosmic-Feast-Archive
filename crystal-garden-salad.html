Crystal Garden Salad

A crisp fresh salad with cool colors, layered textures, and a bright dressing. This page gives variety to the website by balancing heavier dishes with something light and clean.

Time Module

15 min

Skill Level

Easy

Dish Signal

Fresh colors for the orbit table

This is one of the easiest recipe pages to style beautifully because the ingredients are naturally colorful. It also helps the full website feel balanced instead of too heavy.

Ingredient Vault

Ingredients

  • 2 cups lettuce leaves
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 apple, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup purple cabbage, shredded
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons roasted seeds
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Cooking Sequence

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Wash and dry all the vegetables and fruit properly.
  2. Place the lettuce, cucumber, apple, and cabbage in a large bowl.
  3. Sprinkle the feta cheese and roasted seeds over the salad.
  4. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  5. Pour the dressing over the salad just before serving.
  6. Toss gently so the ingredients stay crisp and fresh.
  7. Serve immediately for the best texture.
