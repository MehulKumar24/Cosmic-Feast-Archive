Site Introduction

Why this theme works

The design mixes fantasy storytelling with sci-fi structure. Dark cosmic backgrounds, glowing borders, and elegant typography create a unique identity, while the page layout stays simple enough for strong marks in usability, responsiveness, and clean structure.

Kitchen Notes

How to read the recipes

Each recipe page includes the required title, image, ingredients, step-by-step method, preparation time, and difficulty level. The labels are slightly themed, but the content itself remains straightforward and easy to follow.
