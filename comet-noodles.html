Codex Entry 05

Comet Noodles

A quick noodle bowl with garlic, vegetables, and glossy sauce. It is a useful final page because it adds movement and energy to the collection without being hard to understand.

Time Module

25 min

Skill Level

Medium

Dish Signal

Fast flavor for the after-hours kitchen

This recipe gives the website a quick weeknight option. It also suits the futuristic theme because the noodles and streaks of sauce naturally suggest movement and speed.

Noodles Quick Garlic Chili

Ingredient Vault

Ingredients

  • 200 g noodles
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili sauce
  • 1 teaspoon vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Sequence

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Cook the noodles according to the package instructions, then drain them.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and stir-fry garlic and onion briefly.
  3. Add the vegetables and cook until they stay slightly crisp.
  4. Mix soy sauce, chili sauce, vinegar, salt, and pepper into the pan.
  5. Add the cooked noodles and toss until the sauce coats everything evenly.
  6. Cook for another minute so the noodles absorb the flavor.
  7. Serve hot with extra herbs or spring onion if desired.
