Stardust Flatbread

A warm flatbread topped with roasted vegetables, herbs, and cheese. It works nicely as a shareable snack recipe and gives the archive another visually strong savory card.

Time Module

35 min

Skill Level

Easy

Dish Signal

A bright share-plate from the archive

This flatbread adds variety in both shape and serving style. It helps the home page feel more dynamic because not every recipe card is a bowl or plated meal.

Ingredient Vault

Ingredients

  • 2 flatbreads or naan bases
  • 2 tablespoons pizza sauce
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup bell peppers, sliced
  • 1/4 cup onion, sliced
  • 1/4 cup sweet corn
  • 1 teaspoon dried herbs
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Cooking Sequence

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Place the flatbreads on a baking tray and spread pizza sauce over each one.
  2. Add mozzarella cheese evenly across the surface.
  3. Scatter the sliced peppers, onion, and sweet corn on top.
  4. Drizzle lightly with olive oil and sprinkle dried herbs over the toppings.
  5. Bake until the edges turn crisp and the cheese melts fully.
  6. Remove from the oven and let the flatbread rest briefly.
  7. Slice and serve warm.
