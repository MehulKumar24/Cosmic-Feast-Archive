Aurora Risotto

A creamy mushroom risotto with spinach, garlic, and parmesan. This recipe adds a rich, elegant dinner option to the archive and suits the glowing aurora color palette.

Time Module

45 min

Skill Level

Medium

Dish Signal

A calm, rich centerpiece dish

Aurora Risotto brings a more refined mood to the site. It balances the quicker recipes with something creamy and patient, which helps the archive feel more complete.

Ingredient Vault

Ingredients

  • 1 cup arborio rice
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 cup spinach
  • 4 cups warm vegetable stock
  • 1/3 cup grated parmesan
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Cooking Sequence

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Heat butter and olive oil in a pan, then cook the onion until soft.
  2. Add garlic and mushrooms and stir until the mushrooms begin to brown.
  3. Add the arborio rice and mix for one minute so the grains are coated.
  4. Pour in a small amount of warm stock and stir gently until it is absorbed.
  5. Keep adding stock little by little, stirring often, until the rice becomes creamy.
  6. Add spinach, parmesan, salt, and pepper, then stir until the spinach softens.
  7. Serve hot while the risotto is smooth and glossy.
