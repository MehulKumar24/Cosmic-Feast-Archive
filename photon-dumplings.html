Codex Entry 07

Photon Dumplings

Soft dumplings filled with vegetables, garlic, and sesame flavor. This page adds a more detailed recipe to the site and gives you one higher-difficulty entry for variety.

Time Module

55 min

Skill Level

Hard

Dish Signal

A sharper recipe for advanced cooks

Photon Dumplings add contrast to the collection by introducing folding and steaming. That makes the archive feel broader and gives the site a more complete recipe range.

Steamed Vegetable Handmade

Ingredient Vault

Ingredients

  • 20 dumpling wrappers
  • 1 cup cabbage, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup carrot, grated
  • 1/2 cup spring onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Sequence

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Mix cabbage, carrot, spring onion, garlic, soy sauce, sesame oil, and seasoning.
  2. Place a small spoon of filling in the center of each dumpling wrapper.
  3. Wet the edges lightly with water and fold the wrappers closed.
  4. Pleat the edges or press them firmly so the filling stays sealed inside.
  5. Arrange the dumplings in a steamer basket with a little space between them.
  6. Steam for about 10 to 12 minutes until the wrappers turn glossy and tender.
  7. Serve hot with chili sauce or soy dip.
