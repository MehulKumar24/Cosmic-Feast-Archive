Lunar Herb Pasta

A creamy pasta recipe layered with garlic, herbs, spinach, and parmesan. It keeps the flavor comforting, while the presentation matches the moonlit fantasy theme of the site.

Time Module

30 min

Skill Level

Medium

Dish Signal

Comfort food with a fantasy finish

This dish works well as a featured savory recipe because it is easy to understand, looks rich in an image card, and gives you a nice ingredient list for the assignment page.

Ingredient Vault

Ingredients

  • 200 g pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1 cup cooking cream
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Cooking Sequence

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until tender, then drain and keep it aside.
  2. Heat butter in a pan and cook the garlic for a short time until fragrant.
  3. Add spinach and basil, then stir until the leaves soften.
  4. Pour in the cooking cream and let it warm gently without boiling too hard.
  5. Mix in the parmesan, salt, and pepper until the sauce becomes smooth.
  6. Add the cooked pasta and toss until every piece is coated in the herb sauce.
  7. Serve hot with extra parmesan and basil on top.
