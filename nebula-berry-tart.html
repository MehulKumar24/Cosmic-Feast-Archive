Codex Entry 04

Nebula Berry Tart

A chilled dessert tart with biscuit crust, creamy filling, and berries on top. It gives the site a softer and more magical side while still keeping the recipe structure simple.

Time Module

60 min

Skill Level

Medium

Dish Signal

A dessert card with strong visual impact

Dessert pages are great for creativity marks because the shapes and colors naturally look stylish. This tart also helps your home page card section feel more varied.

Ingredient Vault

Ingredients

  • 200 g biscuit crumbs
  • 80 g melted butter
  • 1 cup cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup thick yogurt or whipped cream
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • 1 tablespoon berry jam or glaze

Cooking Sequence

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Mix the biscuit crumbs with melted butter until the texture is even.
  2. Press the mixture into a tart dish and chill it to form the crust.
  3. In a bowl, beat cream cheese, yogurt or whipped cream, sugar, and vanilla.
  4. Spread the filling over the chilled crust smoothly.
  5. Arrange the mixed berries on top in a neat pattern.
  6. Brush lightly with berry jam or glaze for a shiny finish.
  7. Chill again before slicing and serving.
