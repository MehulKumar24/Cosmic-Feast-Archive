Codex Entry 10

Moonbeam Pancakes

Soft breakfast pancakes with vanilla, banana, and a light drizzle on top. This page adds a morning recipe to the archive and makes the full collection feel more complete.

Time Module

20 min

Skill Level

Easy

Dish Signal

A gentle breakfast entry for the codex

Moonbeam Pancakes widen the recipe mix beyond lunch and dinner dishes. They also add a lighter dessert-breakfast crossover card to the home page.

Breakfast Sweet Quick

Ingredient Vault

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 ripe banana, mashed
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  • Butter or oil for the pan

Cooking Sequence

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder in a bowl.
  2. In another bowl, whisk the mashed banana, egg, milk, and vanilla.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients until a smooth batter forms.
  4. Heat a lightly greased pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour small amounts of batter onto the pan and cook until bubbles appear.
  6. Flip each pancake and cook the other side until golden.
  7. Stack and serve warm with honey, fruit, or syrup.
