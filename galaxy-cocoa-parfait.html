Codex Entry 12

Galaxy Cocoa Parfait

A chilled layered dessert with cocoa cream, biscuit crumbs, and fruit. This final page gives the archive a dramatic dessert finish and rounds the collection out to 12 recipes.

Time Module

25 min

Skill Level

Easy

Dish Signal

A deep dessert ending for the collection

Galaxy Cocoa Parfait works well as the final codex entry because it feels rich, elegant, and visually dramatic without needing a complicated method.

Dessert Layered Cocoa
Back to Home

Ingredient Vault

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whipped cream
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 cup biscuit crumbs
  • 1/4 cup chopped berries or banana
  • Chocolate shavings for topping

Cooking Sequence

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Mix the whipped cream, cocoa powder, and sugar until smooth.
  2. Place a layer of biscuit crumbs at the bottom of a dessert glass.
  3. Add a layer of cocoa cream over the crumbs.
  4. Scatter a little fruit on top for freshness.
  5. Repeat the layers until the glass is filled.
  6. Finish with chocolate shavings and chill before serving.
